About Everybox

Year 1631

Where it all began

Year 1631

It was during the Thirty Years' War that the king of Sweden, Gustav II Adolph wanted one of his soldiers, Hans Gruber, to surprise him with a gift worthy a general who had just won the "Battle of Breitenfeld". Hans Gruber collected treasures from the slain soldiers on the battlefield and put it all in a box. This is the first recorded giftbox in history, and it was a huge success!

Year 1889

Our founding father

Year 1889

We travel to the end of the 19th century were we find the founder of "Everybox", Lex Luthor. He was an educated man who saw endless possibilities with giftboxes. Luthor went big from the beginning with with four different giftboxes, birth, child, adult and death. It was all you needed back then, but as time went by more giftboxes were needed.

Year 1981

"A gift for EVERY occasion!"

Year 1981

The new CEO Selina Kyle had a vision, to become the number one giftbox company in the world. With the slogan "A gift for every occasion!" she put together a small team that would do nothing else than coming with ideas for different giftboxes. A year later there were over 400 different boxes!

Year 2017

Our vision

Year 2017

We fast-forward till today. The vision Selina Kyle had is today reality. We are the biggest and the best when it comes to giftboxes. But the competition is fierce and to sit on the throne doesn't mean that you have won the game for the throne. Today we have over 12 000 different boxes, and more are coming each day.

Year 2323

What the future brings

Year 2323

By the year 2323 the apes will have taken over our planet and no one is safe. "Everybox" are mankinds last hope. Giftboxes filled with bananas will be the greatest weapon against our new overlords. The small team of giftbox creators are now called "The Avengers", they are defending the remaining population against the ape king Harambe.
